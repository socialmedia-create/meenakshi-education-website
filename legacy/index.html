Our Legacy

75 Years of Educational Pioneering in Chennai

· · ·

The Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions is not simply a collection of colleges and schools. It is a living legacy — built over 75+ years by a family whose deepest conviction is that education is the most transformative gift one generation can offer the next.

As one of Chennai's most respected educational pioneers, MSGI has grown from a single founder's vision into a full-spectrum educational group — spanning Engineering, Management, Women's Higher Education, and International Schooling. Through every era of change, one constant has remained: an uncompromising commitment to quality, character, and student success.

This is the story of that legacy — told through the founder who dared to dream, the daughters who expanded the dream, and the third generation that is reimagining it for a global world.

A Legacy Measured in Lives Transformed

Every student who has walked through the doors of an MSGI institution carries forward a piece of this legacy. Engineers who lead industries, managers who drive innovation, women who break barriers, and global thinkers who were once students in our classrooms.

The Meenakshi Sundararajan legacy is not written in stone — it is written in careers launched, potential realised, and communities strengthened.

Founder's Story

The Visionary Founder — K.R. Sundararajan

· · ·

Every great institution begins with a single act of faith — the belief that what is being built today will matter to generations not yet born. For the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions, that act of faith belonged to K.R. Sundararajan.

A true educational pioneer in Chennai, K.R. Sundararajan saw education not as a commodity to be delivered, but as a mission to be lived. In an era when quality educational institutions were few, he made a defining choice: to build an institution that would serve the community with integrity, nurture students with purpose, and stand for excellence long after he was gone.

"He believed that a nation's true wealth was not in its resources, but in the quality of minds it cultivated — and he set out to cultivate the finest."

His founding philosophy rested on four pillars — knowledge, discipline, innovation, and societal impact. These were not abstract ideals but the daily standards by which every institution under his name was built and measured. From curriculum design to campus culture, from faculty selection to student welfare — every decision reflected his conviction that education is, at its core, an act of service to the next generation.

K.R. Sundararajan's vision was not merely to create a school or a college. It was to create an educational legacy in Chennai — one that would grow, adapt, and deepen with each passing generation. That vision has not only endured; it has flourished.

The true test of any institutional legacy is not what its founder built — but what those who follow choose to preserve and amplify. At MSGI, each successive generation has honoured the founding vision while expanding its reach, depth, and impact.

Second Generation Leadership

Carrying the Vision Forward — Dr. K.S. Babai & Dr. K.S. Lakshmi

Secretary

As one of the daughters of founder K.R. Sundararajan, Dr. K.S. Babai stepped into leadership with the same dedication to academic excellence that defined her father's work. She played a pivotal role in expanding the group's institutional presence in Chennai, strengthening governance structures, and ensuring that quality education remained the non-negotiable foundation of every institution under MSGI. Under her leadership, the group's reputation for academic rigour and student-focused outcomes was firmly cemented.

Secretary

Dr. K.S. Lakshmi brought both academic depth and institutional vision to the second generation of MSGI leadership. Together, the two sisters transformed MSGI from a strong regional institution into one of Chennai's most trusted educational groups.
