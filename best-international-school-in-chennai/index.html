IB Diploma Programme (IBDP)

The IB Diploma Programme is a rigorous, internationally acclaimed qualification for Grades 11 and 12. Students study six subjects across language, sciences, humanities, arts, and mathematics — alongside the Extended Essay (EE), Theory of Knowledge (TOK), and Creativity, Activity, Service (CAS). The IBDP is accepted by universities in over 150 countries and is widely regarded as the gold standard of pre-university education.
