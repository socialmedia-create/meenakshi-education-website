TEDxTNS Youth – TEDx Event in Chennai

TEDxTNS Youth is a TEDx event hosted at The NEST School, part of the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions, bringing together young innovators and powerful TEDx Talks in Chennai.

What is
TEDx ?

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group.

TEDxTNS Youth is an independently organized TED event hosted by The NEST School, Kodambakkam, Chennai - 60. The event brings together bright young minds and changemakers to share powerful stories that challenge perspectives and ignite local action.

What is TEDxTNS Youth?

Theme : Edge of Becoming

At the edge of becoming lies a moment of transformation—where ideas evolve, identities shift, and new possibilities emerge. It is the space between who we are and who we are meant to become.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is TEDxTNS Youth?

TEDxTNS Youth is an independently organized TEDx event hosted at The NEST School, Chennai. It brings together young innovators, thinkers, and storytellers to share powerful ideas under the theme "Edge of Becoming".

Where is The NEST School (TNS) located?

The NEST School is located in Kodambakkam, Chennai, within the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions (MSGI) campus. It is a centrally accessible location in the city.

What curriculum does The NEST School offer?

The NEST School offers internationally recognized curricula including IB (International Baccalaureate) and Cambridge (IGCSE), focusing on global learning and holistic development.

Who can apply to speak at TEDxTNS Youth?

Students, young innovators, and individuals with impactful ideas are encouraged to apply. If your idea can inspire change, challenge perspectives, or spark meaningful conversations, this platform is for you.

What makes an idea “TEDx worthy”?

A TEDx-worthy idea is original, thought-provoking, and meaningful. It should have the power to inspire, educate, or shift perspectives—not just inform, but create impact.

How do I submit my talk?

You need to submit your speaker profile, topic, and a 1-minute talk video via Google Drive. Ensure your video access is set to "Anyone with the link".

When is the event happening?

TEDxTNS Youth will take place on July 24, 2026 in Chennai.

Is this an official TED event?

TEDxTNS Youth is independently organized under a TED license. It follows TED guidelines while being hosted locally by The NEST School.
