Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in nursing education, providing transformative learning experiences that integrate compassionate care with advanced clinical technology.

Our Mission

To develop skilled, professional, and ethical nurses through rigorous academic programs, high-quality clinical training, and community-centric health services.

Academic Programs

Choose the path that defines your medical career.

Undergraduate 4 Years Full Time

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc)

A foundational degree focusing on basic nursing sciences, patient care, community health, and medical ethics.

Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB & English (Min 45%)
Age: 17 to 35 Years
Postgraduate 2 Years Full Time

Master of Science in Nursing (M.Sc)

Advanced specialization for registered nurses in Medical-Surgical, Pediatrics, OBG, or Psychiatric nursing.

Eligibility: B.Sc Nursing + 1 Year Experience
Specializations: 5 Major Tracks
