Judging Framework

Evaluation Criteria

We are not looking for flashy slides. We are looking for founders in the making.

01
Problem Clarity
How clearly and deeply the problem is defined, validated, and understood with supporting evidence.
02
Innovation Quotient
The uniqueness, originality, and creative leap of the proposed solution in the market.
03
Feasibility
Practical viability of building and implementing the idea with available or acquirable resources.
04
Impact Potential
The potential to create meaningful, real-world value and disrupt an existing market or behavior.
05
Team Strength
The capability, clarity, commitment, and complementary skills of the founding team behind the idea.

Frequently Asked Questions - Startup Summit 2026

What is Startup Summit 2026 at MSGI?

Startup Summit 2026 is an innovation and entrepreneurship event in Chennai where students and aspiring entrepreneurs can pitch ideas, gain mentorship, and connect with industry experts.

Who can participate in the Startup Summit?

Students, startups, and innovators from any background can participate individually or as a team.

How can I register for Startup Summit 2026?

You can register through the official Startup Summit page by filling out the registration form.

Where is the event conducted?

The event will be held at Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College, Chennai.

Will there be funding opportunities?

Yes, selected startups will get opportunities to pitch to investors and receive funding and incubation support.

Do I need a fully developed idea to participate?

No, you can participate even if your idea is in the early stage. We welcome innovative concepts at all levels and help you refine and develop them.

Who can apply for this startup summit?

Students, aspiring entrepreneurs, and innovators from any background can apply. If you have a unique idea or passion to build something, you are welcome.

What kind of support will I receive?

Selected participants will receive mentorship from industry experts, guidance from startup founders, and opportunities to improve their business model and pitch.

Will there be funding opportunities?

Yes, shortlisted startups will get opportunities to pitch their ideas to investors and may receive funding or incubation support based on their potential.

What happens after I submit my idea?

Your idea will be reviewed by our panel. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for further discussions, mentoring sessions, and pitch opportunities.

Will I get a chance to interact with industry experts?

Yes, you will have opportunities to interact with startup founders, mentors, and industry professionals through panel discussions and networking sessions.

Is there any registration fee?

No, participation is completely free. Our goal is to support and encourage innovation among aspiring entrepreneurs.
