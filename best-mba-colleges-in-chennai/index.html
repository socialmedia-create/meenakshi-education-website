About MSSM

25 Years of Building Business Leaders

Meenakshi Sundararajan School of Management has spent over a quarter century developing management professionals who lead, innovate, and contribute across every sector of India's economy. As one of the best MBA colleges in Chennai, MSSM combines the academic foundation of a University of Madras affiliation with a deeply practical, industry-engaged approach to management education.

Our institution is built on a simple but powerful conviction: that great management education shapes not just careers, but character. At MSSM, students develop the analytical depth, leadership agility, and professional confidence to thrive in dynamic corporate environments — from Day 1 of their careers to the boardroom.

Part of the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions — which has served Chennai's educational community for over 75 years — MSSM carries the institutional trust, academic rigour, and community commitment that distinguishes a truly excellent management college from merely a good one.
