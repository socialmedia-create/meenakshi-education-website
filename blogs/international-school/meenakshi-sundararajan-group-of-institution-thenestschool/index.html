About MSGI

The Nest School is Part of Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions

The Nest School proudly stands as the school-level institution under the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions (MSGI) — one of Chennai's most trusted and revered educational ecosystems with over 75 years of unbroken academic excellence.

Nestled in Kodambakkam, Chennai, the MSGI campus has been a beacon of learning for five generations of students — from the Baby Boomers who first walked its corridors to the Gen Alpha learners of today. When you choose The Nest School, you are not simply choosing a school — you are joining a living legacy that has shaped thousands of leaders, innovators, and changemakers.
