About The Nest School

Founded on Vision.
Rooted in Legacy.

The Nest School was founded in 2022 by Deepa Umamaheshwaran and Sreekanth, visionaries who believed Chennai deserved an international school that fused global best practices with the proven values of MSGI's 75-year educational heritage.

As a proud member of the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions, The Nest School inherits a culture of academic rigour, character development, and community trust that has shaped 3,00,000+ lives since 1950. The result is an international school that is both globally credentialed and deeply grounded.

Where is The Nest
School Located?

The Nest School is situated within the prestigious MSGI campus in Kodambakkam, Chennai — one of the city's most central and accessible locations. Students benefit from a shared 15-acre green campus that is also home to MSEC's world-class facilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need
to Know

The Nest School uniquely combines the global rigour of IB and Cambridge curricula with MSGI's unmatched 75-year educational legacy. Unlike standalone international schools, Nest students benefit from a mature institutional ecosystem, experienced educators, world-class campus infrastructure including 100+ labs and an incubation centre, a culture of innovation (AI, robotics, sustainability), and an extensive sports programme. This holistic environment makes The Nest School the standout choice for international education in Chennai.
The Nest School is located within the MSGI campus in Kodambakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu — a central, highly accessible neighbourhood well-connected by metro, bus, and road. The school sits on a lush 15-acre campus shared with MSGI's other institutions, giving students access to expansive green spaces, state-of-the-art labs, sports courts, and the innovation hub.
The Nest School offers four internationally recognised programmes: IB Early Years Programme (EYP) for ages 3–6, IB Primary Years Programme (PYP) for Grades 1–5, Cambridge IGCSE for Grades 6–10, and a choice between Cambridge A Levels and the IB Diploma Programme for Grades 11–12. This comprehensive, globally recognised pathway ensures students can move seamlessly into top universities around the world.
