Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries regarding the 2026 admissions at The Nest School.

When do admissions open for 2026?
Admissions for the academic year 2026 are currently open. Parents can register online through our website or visit the campus to initiate the process early, as seats are limited.
What curriculum does The Nest School offer?
The Nest School proudly offers globally recognized programs, specifically the IB (International Baccalaureate) and Cambridge curriculums, ensuring a world-class educational standard.
How to apply for admission?
You can apply by filling out the online enquiry form on our website. Once submitted, our admission counsellor will contact you to guide you through the next steps, including a campus visit.
Is there an entrance test?
For pre-primary levels, we conduct a friendly interaction session. For higher grades, there is a basic grade-level assessment to understand the student's proficiency in core subjects to ensure a smooth transition.
Where is The Nest School located?
The Nest School is centrally located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Our 15-acre campus is easily accessible from all major residential areas in the city.
What facilities are available on campus?
Our campus features modern smart classrooms, extensive sports infrastructure (football, basketball, swimming), dedicated arts and music studios, well-equipped science and computer labs, and 24/7 security.
