Common Questions

MSGI — Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions — is known for delivering holistic, quality education across disciplines. From producing industry-ready engineers at MSEC to grooming global business leaders at MSSM and nurturing internationally-minded learners at The Nest School, MSGI has built a strong reputation for academic excellence and student outcomes in Chennai.

MSGI spans multiple disciplines: MSEC offers B.E./B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech engineering programs; MSSM offers MBA and PGDM management programs; The Nest School delivers IB and Cambridge curricula from Pre-KG to Grade 12; and MCW offers undergraduate and postgraduate arts and science programs.

Yes. The Nest School under MSGI offers both IB (International Baccalaureate) and Cambridge programs. It is designed for globally-oriented families who want their children to develop critical thinking, multilingual communication, and a love for learning in a safe and nurturing campus environment in Chennai.

All MSGI campuses are situated in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Each campus is well-connected by road and public transport, offering modern facilities including on-campus hostels, libraries, labs, and sports infrastructure designed for a complete campus experience.

Applying is straightforward. Visit cick to register, select the institution of your choice, complete the online application, and upload required documents. You can also call the admissions helpline or visit any MSGI campus in person. Early applications are strongly encouraged as seats are limited.
