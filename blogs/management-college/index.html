About the Institution

Meenakshi Sundararajan School of Management

Meenakshi Sundararajan School of Management (MSSM) is a premier management institution located in Kodambakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. As part of the Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions (MSGI) — one of South India's most trusted educational groups with over 75 years of excellence — MSSM blends legacy with innovation.

MSSM is committed to developing industry-ready management professionals equipped with analytical thinking, leadership ability, and practical business acumen. The school's MBA programme is designed to meet the evolving demands of the global corporate landscape.

  • Established institution under MSGI's 75+ year educational legacy
  • Located centrally in Kodambakkam, Chennai — well accessible across the city
  • Industry-aligned MBA curriculum with AI-ready learning pathways
  • Strong focus on corporate exposure, leadership and real-world readiness
  • Dedicated placement cell with active corporate partnerships
  • Regular conclaves, guest lectures & MoU-driven industry tie-ups
