The MSSM Placement Journey — How It Works

Structured, transparent, and student-centred — here is how MSSM prepares every student from enrolment to offer letter.

1

Orientation & Career Mapping

From the first week, MSCDC conducts career orientation — mapping each student's background, strengths, and goals to the most suitable MBA specialization and industry pathway.

2

Semester-Wise Skill Building

Each semester, students undergo structured training in aptitude, communication, domain knowledge, and corporate readiness — progressively deepening their employability profile.

3

Industry Exposure & Internship

Live projects, corporate visits, and a structured internship programme give students verifiable professional experience before they sit for campus placements.

4

Pre-Placement Intensive

In the lead-up to placement season, MSCDC runs intensive mock interviews, GD sessions, and aptitude tests — tailored to each student's target companies and roles.

5

Campus Placement Drives

MSSM hosts on-campus recruitment drives with employers across BFSI, IT, FMCG, consulting, and manufacturing — bringing the job market directly to students in Chennai.
