The Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions (MSGI) has been a beacon of quality education in Chennai since 1950. With a 15-acre green campus in Kodambakkam and over 3,00,000 students shaped across generations, MSGI's impact on Tamil Nadu's education landscape is unparalleled.

MSGI proudly runs 4 premier institutions, each nurturing a distinct vision of excellence — from engineering and management to school-level education.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Registering for MSEC is straightforward. Visit the official MSGI website and navigate to the Admissions section. Fill in the online application form with your Class 10 & 12 details, upload required documents, and submit. You can also walk into the admissions office at Kodambakkam, Chennai, for in-person guidance. Admissions are based on Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) counselling.
Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College (MSEC) is located in Kodambakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu – a central, well-connected location. The 15-acre green campus is easily accessible by MTC buses, Chennai Metro, and private transport. The address is Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College, Kodambakkam, Chennai – 600 024.
MSEC offers a wide array of undergraduate B.E./B.Tech programs including Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Information Technology. At the postgraduate level, M.E. programs and MBA are also available. All programs are affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE.
MSEC boasts exceptional infrastructure across its 15-acre campus. Highlights include 100+ state-of-the-art laboratories, a 100-seater startup incubation center, 10 industry centers of excellence, a well-stocked central library with digital resources, smart classrooms, sports facilities, canteen, and separate hostel accommodations for boys and girls.
MSEC has a dedicated Career Development Cell (MSCDC) that provides 100+ hours of aptitude and technical training, communication skills, mock drives, and industry mentorship. Top companies from IT, manufacturing, BFSI, and core engineering sectors recruit MSEC graduates every year. The strong alumni network of 5,000+ professionals further enhances placement prospects through referrals and mentorship.
MSGI – Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions – is a premier educational group founded in 1950 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. With 75+ years of excellence and 3,00,000+ students shaped, MSGI runs four institutions: MSEC (Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College), MSSM (School of Management), The Nest School, and MCW (Meenakshi College for Women). Each institution upholds MSGI's core commitment to quality education, character building, and career readiness.
