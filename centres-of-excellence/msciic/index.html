About CIIC

CIIC is committed to transforming students into industry-ready professionals by enabling experiential learning, industry exposure, innovation ecosystems, corporate engagement, and technology-driven skill development.

The centre supports students from Engineering, Management, and School education ecosystems through structured interventions that enhance employability, entrepreneurship, research, and practical learning.

Innovation Hub

Industry Centre of Excellence (ICE)

The ICE, functioning under CIIC, is a future-focused experiential learning ecosystem designed to expose students to emerging technologies and industrial practices.

ICE acts as a common interdisciplinary technology hub supporting school students, engineering graduates, and industry professionals alike.

  • ▶ Hands-on learning focus
  • ▶ Multidisciplinary innovation
  • ▶ Prototype development
  • ▶ Hackathons and challenges
