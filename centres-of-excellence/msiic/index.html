MSGI Launches Integrated Incubation Ecosystem with Campus Angels

MSGI × Campus Angels Partnership

Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions (MSGI) has partnered with Campus Angels to build a structured entrepreneurship ecosystem that supports students from ideation to venture creation.

Through this joint initiative, startups will be guided through pre-incubation and incubation, with high-potential ventures gaining access to mentorship, industry networks, and funding opportunities.

Our Vision & Mission

The Meenakshi Sundararajan Innovation Incubation Center (MS-IIC) is established with the vision to foster an environment where young minds can transform innovative ideas into reality, driving entrepreneurship and technological advancement. MS-IIC aims to be a catalyst for creating sustainable business solutions and fostering collaborations between academia, industry, and entrepreneurial ecosystems.
