About the Group

One of the Top Educational Institutions in Chennai — Trusted for 75+ Years

Our Heritage

75+ Years of Educational Excellence in Chennai

Among all educational institutions in Chennai, very few can claim a legacy that spans over seven decades and continues to grow stronger. The Meenakshi Sundararajan Group of Institutions was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that quality education, delivered with purpose, changes lives and transforms communities.

1950s

The Foundation

MSGI is established in Chennai, beginning a mission of quality education that would span generations and disciplines.

1990s

Engineering & Management Education

MSEC and MSSM are launched, marking MSGI's entry into technical and management education — and building two institutions that now rank among the best in Chennai.

2000s

Women Empowerment & International Education

The Women's College and The Nest International School are established, completing MSGI's vision of a comprehensive educational ecosystem for all learners.

Today

One of Chennai's Most Trusted Groups

MSGI serves thousands of students every year — in engineering, management, arts & science, and international school education — all under one roof of trust.

Why 75 Years of Legacy Matters

The Meenakshi Sundararajan Group's 75-year track record is the strongest signal of institutional quality. It means every degree, every school certificate, and every diploma issued under this group carries the weight of proven credibility — recognised by employers, universities, and communities across India and globally.

When you choose any institution under MSGI, you are not just choosing a college or school — you are joining a tradition of excellence that has already shaped thousands of successful careers across engineering, business, the arts, and global education.
